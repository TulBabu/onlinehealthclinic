COVID 19

COVID 19 also known as Corona virus disease 19 / SARS COV-2 is a communicable disease came from Wuhan province of China and was declared as pandemic on March 11 by WHO. Till date, it has infected more than 2.2million people globally and killed more than 1.5lakhs of people.

Transmission

Human to Human via Respiratory Droplet while coughing, sneezing, singing, talking

Sign /Symptom
  • Fever
  • Dry cough
  • Muscle ache
  • Fatigue
  • Shortness of breath
Prevention
  • Hand washing with soap and water frequently.
  • Maintain at least 1meter distance preferably 2meters.
  • Avoid going out unnecessarily
  • Avoid touching door knobs, railing etc. unnecessarily
  • Use antiseptics
  • Eating Nutritious food etc.
